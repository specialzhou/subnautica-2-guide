Subnautica 2 is a deep-sea survival exploration game developed by Unknown Worlds and Krafton, launching into Early Access on May 14, 2026. It sold 2 million copies in the first 12 hours, peaked at 467,582 concurrent players on Steam (all-time rank #29), and hit 413,200 simultaneous Twitch viewers.

Building on the original's beloved exploration formula, Subnautica 2 drops you on a brand-new alien ocean planet — now with up to 4-player co-op. Starting from a crashed escape pod with no map and no quest markers, you'll survive by crafting gear, building a base, and exploring the unknown depths.

💡 A word for new players
No quest markers is intentional — and it's the best part. Don't panic, don't rush into the deep. Master the shallows first, and gear will unlock naturally as you go.

Subnautica 2 是 Unknown Worlds 和 Krafton 联合开发的深海生存探索游戏，于 2026年5月14日 进入抢先体验（Early Access）。上市12小时内售出 200万份，Steam 历史峰值在线 467,582人，Twitch 同时观看峰值 413,200人，登上 Steam 历史并发排行第29位。

游戏延续第一代深海探索的核心体验，在全新的外星海洋星球上进行，并首次加入最多 4人联机 模式。你将从一个坠落的逃生舱开始，在没有地图、没有任务箭头的情况下，靠自己的探索在未知深海中生存下去。

First Hour Checklist第一小时清单

Step-by-step roadmap from landing to getting stable从落地到站稳脚跟的完整路线图

Not sure what to do at the start? Here's a minute-by-minute action plan to get you through the most dangerous early phase.

很多新手开局不知道该做什么，这里给你一个分钟级的行动计划，照着做就能度过最危险的早期阶段。

  • 0–3 min
    Open every locker in the escape pod. Grab everything — especially the medkit and nutrition bar. These are your first lifelines.
    打开逃生舱里所有储物箱，拿走全部物品。特别注意：急救包、营养棒一定要拿，这是你最早的续命道具。
  • 3–5 min
    Press Tab to open the PDA. Read all survival alerts — the PDA is your quest log. There's no quest tracker in this game.
    按 Tab 打开 PDA，读完所有生存警报。游戏里没有任务栏，PDA 就是你的任务书。
  • 5–15 min
    Dive in the 0–30m zone near the pod. Collect orange rocks (Titanium) and green-spotted rocks (Copper Ore), at least 5 of each. This depth is safe — no oxygen worries.
    在逃生舱附近 0–30米 范围下水，捡起橙色岩石（钛矿石）和绿斑岩石（铜矿），各捡5块以上。这个深度很安全，不用担心氧气。
  • 15–20 min
    Craft a Standard O₂ Tank (Titanium ×1) in your fabricator. Equip it immediately — extends dive time right away. This is the #1 priority craft.
    用随身合成器合成：标准氧气罐（钛×1）。马上装备，潜水时间立刻延长。这是全游戏第一优先级合成。
  • 20–25 min
    Craft a Knife (Titanium ×1 + Silicone ×1). Silicone comes from the orange mushroom-like plants near the pod — harvest by hand. The knife lets you collect plant samples and fend off small fish.
    合成刀具（钛×1 + 硅胶×1）。硅胶来自逃生舱附近的橙色蘑菇状植物，用手直接采摘。刀可以采集植物，也能击退小鱼。
  • 25–35 min
    Place a Beacon (Copper ×1 + Titanium ×1) outside your pod so you can always find home. Many beginners get lost on their very first dive.
    在逃生舱外放置一个信标（Beacon）（铜×1 + 钛×1），防止你以后找不到回家的路。这一步很重要，很多新手第一次探索就迷路了。
  • 35–50 min
    Craft a Scanner (Titanium ×1 + Battery ×1). Battery needs Copper ×2 + Acid Mushroom ×2. Start scanning creatures and plants — each scan unlocks PDA entries and sometimes new blueprints.
    合成扫描仪（钛×1 + 电池×1），电池需要 铜×2 + 酸性蘑菇×2。开始扫描周围的生物和植物，扫描完成后 PDA 会解锁相关条目，有时会触发新图纸。
  • 50–60 min
    Find a flat patch of seabed and place your first Base Foundation. Don't worry about the perfect spot — early bases can always be rebuilt. The goal is to start accumulating crafting stations.
    找一块相对平坦的海底，放置你的第一个基础舱（Base Foundation）。别担心选址不完美——早期基地随时可以拆了重建，关键是现在就开始积累合成台。
💡 By end of hour one you should have
Standard O₂ Tank, Knife, Scanner, 1× Beacon, and the start of your first base. If you have all of these, your start is a success.
💡 第一小时结束后你应该拥有
标准氧气罐、刀具、扫描仪、信标（×1个）、第一个基础舱雏形。如果这些都有了，你的开局是成功的。

1. Beginner Basics — Core Gameplay1. 新手入门 — 游戏基础

What to do first · HUD explained · Key controls开局做什么 · 界面说明 · 按键指南

First 5 Things To Do刚开始要做的 5 件事

  1. Search every locker in the escape pod and grab the medkit, nutrition bar, and basic materials.检查逃生舱内的所有储物箱，拿走急救包、营养棒和基础材料。
  2. Press Tab to open the PDA and read all survival alerts — this is your mission log.按 Tab 打开 PDA，阅读系统自动发送的生存警报，了解当前优先任务。
  3. Dive in the shallows (0–30m) and collect Quartz, Titanium, and Copper — these craft everything.在舱外浅海（0–30 米）采集石英、钛矿石、铜矿，这是合成一切的基础。
  4. Craft an O₂ Tank (Titanium ×1) — your #1 priority. Extends dive time immediately.合成氧气罐（需要钛×1），这是最优先的合成目标，马上扩充潜水时间。
  5. Place a Beacon near your pod so you can always find your way back.在逃生舱周围放置一个信标，防止迷路找不到回家的路。

Blue bar, right side. Drink filtered water to refill. Depleting it is more dangerous than food — don't ignore it.右侧蓝色条。喝水或净化海水补充，耗尽比食物更危险。

2. Oxygen & Survival Management2. 氧气与生存管理

Dive time · Food & water · How to stop drowning潜水时间 · 食物饮水 · 不再溺死的秘诀

The #1 cause of death in Subnautica 2 is drowning, not creatures. Manage your oxygen and you'll survive almost anything.

Subnautica 2 最容易死亡的原因是溺死，而不是被生物攻击。管好氧气，你就能活很久。

⚠️ Most Common Beginner Death
Getting absorbed in exploration and forgetting to check your oxygen. By the time you notice it's nearly gone, it's too late to surface. Start ascending when oxygen hits 30% — make it a habit.
⚠️ 新手最常见死法
探索太投入忘看氧气条，等发现只剩一点点时已经来不及上浮。氧气条低于30%就要往上游，养成习惯。

💡 How to craft Glass?
Glass = Quartz ×2, made in your personal fabricator. Quartz is the white transparent crystal scattered on shallow sandy floors — very easy to spot.
💡 玻璃怎么合成？
玻璃 = 石英 ×2，在随身合成器就能做。石英是白色半透明晶体，在浅海沙地大量分布，很好认。

Food & Water Guide食物与饮水指南

Early game (0–1 hr): Rely on pod nutrition bars. Grab the orange berries floating in shallow water (eat directly), and drink seawater filtered through a Filter Bottle.

Mid-game (1–5 hr): Build a Bioreactor in your base and grow alien mushrooms. Once set up, food is no longer a concern.

早期（0–1小时）： 靠逃生舱里的营养棒撑一段时间。同时捡浅海里漂着的橙色浆果（直接食用），喝用过滤瓶净化的海水。

中期（1–5小时）： 在基地里建生物反应舱，种植外星菌菇。一旦建好，食物问题永久解决。

⚠️ Never Drink Raw Seawater
The alien ocean is hyper-saline — drinking it raw accelerates dehydration! Always filter through a Filter Bottle (Copper ×1 + Titanium ×1 + Silicone ×1) first.
⚠️ 不要直接喝海水
外星星球海水盐度极高，直接喝会加速脱水！必须用过滤瓶（铜×1 + 钛×1 + 硅胶×1）净化后再喝。

Advanced gear and vehicle upgrades. End-game essential for deep-dive equipment.高级装备和载具改装在这里做。后期必备，用于制作深层装备。

8. Beginner FAQ8. 新手常见问题

Direct answers to the most common questions最高频问题的直接解答
I keep drowning — what am I doing wrong?我一直在溺死，怎么办？+

Two golden rules:
① Surface when oxygen hits 30% — don't keep mining those last few rocks;
② Craft the High Cap O₂ Tank (Titanium ×3 + Glass ×1) — it gives 3× more dive time. Upgrade ASAP.
Also: use the surface → breathe → dive rhythm. Don't try to maximize every single dive.

溺死是新手最常见的死法。记住两条铁则：
① 氧气低于30%立刻往上浮，不要再贪心继续采集；
② 合成高容量氧气罐（钛×3 + 玻璃×1）比标准罐多3倍时间，一定要尽早升级。
另外，水面换气是免费的，多用"出水→换气→再下去"的节奏，不要一次潜太久。

There are no quest markers — where do I go?游戏没有任务箭头，我该去哪里？+

Subnautica 2's design philosophy is "explore for yourself" — no markers is intentional. The right approach:
① Open the PDA (Tab) — all story clues are inside, updated whenever you scan a wreck or creature;
② Follow the wreckage — ship debris on the surface and in shallow water triggers new content when approached;
③ Just swim and enjoy — the best finds are accidental. Embrace the exploration.

Subnautica 2 的设计理念就是"自己探索"，没有任务箭头是故意的。正确的探索顺序：
① 读 PDA（Tab键）——所有故事线索都在里面，每次扫描残骸和生物都会更新；
② 跟着残骸走——水面和浅海会有大量坠落的飞船残骸，走近就会触发新内容；
③ 不要想太多，随便游就行——好东西都是"意外"发现的，享受探索本身。

I'm terrified of the creatures — how do I cope?被大型生物吓到不敢下水怎么办？+

Completely normal — the developers deliberately designed thalassophobia. Practical tips:
① Use your lights — darkness is the biggest fear source, always keep the Seamoth headlights on;
② Carry decoys — Sound Decoys (Magnetite ×1 + Plant ×1) distract most mid-tier creatures;
③ Scan everything — the PDA shows behavior patterns after scanning; knowing what triggers it reduces fear drastically;
④ Most creatures don't aggress first — leave them alone and they'll leave you alone.

这是正常反应！开发者专门设计了"深海恐惧感"。几个降低恐惧的实用技巧：
① 开灯——黑暗本身就是恐惧来源，随时开海蛾车灯；
② 带诱饵——声诱饵（磁铁矿×1 + 植物×1）能把大部分中型生物引开；
③ 了解生物——扫描仪扫过的生物会在 PDA 里显示习性，知道它吃什么才会攻击，恐惧感会大幅降低；
④ 大部分生物不会主动攻击——你先不碰它，它一般不咬你。

Is multiplayer or solo better for beginners?联机和单机哪个更适合新手？+

Both work — each has trade-offs:
Solo: More immersive, you control the pace, no one takes your resources, and the fear factor is legendary (in a good way).
Multiplayer: Resources accumulate 4× faster, teammates divide the danger, large creatures less frightening. Better for players who don't enjoy isolation.
Recommendation: Solo first playthrough for the story experience, then co-op for a fresh run with role specialization.

两种都可以，但各有优缺点：
单机： 体验更完整，自己掌控节奏，不用担心别人拿走你的资源，恐惧感更强烈（褒义）。
联机： 资源积累快4倍，有队友分工更安全，大型生物遇到时不那么怕，适合不喜欢孤独感的玩家。
建议：第一次玩先单机体验剧情，二周目再联机体验全新分工乐趣。

My base is flooding — what do I do?我的基地进水了怎么办？+

Flooding means Hull Integrity hit zero. Fix steps:
① Build Reinforcement Panels (Titanium ×3 + Glass ×1 = +7 integrity each) to restore structure;
② Use a Repair Tool to right-click and fix each crack;
③ If heavily flooded, swim out first, add panels, then repair the cracks;
④ Every 100m of depth costs ~1 integrity — always build reinforcement panels before expanding a deep base.

基地进水说明结构完整性降到0了。解决步骤：
① 先建造加固隔板（钛×3 + 玻璃×1）补强结构，每个提供 +7 完整性；
② 用修复工具在漏水点右键修复裂缝；
③ 如果大面积进水，先游出基地，加足够的加固隔板后再修复漏洞；
④ 基地深度每增加100米就消耗约1点完整性，注意提前准备加固隔板再建深层基地。

How do I handle the night cycle?游戏里到了晚上怎么应对？+

Subnautica 2 has a day/night cycle. At night the underwater world goes pitch black, and fear doubles. How to cope:
① Stay at base at night — organize inventory, craft gear, wait for dawn;
② If you must go out, turn on all lights and bring Flares (throwable for area lighting);
③ Some creatures are actually quieter at night — experienced players exploit this, but beginners shouldn't;
④ Certain deep creatures have different night behaviors — PDA entries reveal these patterns.

Subnautica 2 有昼夜循环，夜晚水下一片漆黑，恐惧感翻倍。应对方法：
① 夜晚留在基地——整理物资、合成装备，等天亮再出发；
② 如果要出门，开全部灯光，随身携带荧光棒（丢出去照明）；
③ 夜晚有一些生物反而更安静，有经验的玩家会利用夜晚探索——但新手不建议。
④ 某些深层区域的生物在夜晚有不同的行为模式，扫描仪可以查到。

I can't unlock blueprints — how does it work?图纸怎么解锁？我合成不了很多东西。+

Three ways to unlock blueprints:
① Scanner on wreck fragments — find crashed ship debris underwater, scan 4 fragments of the same type to unlock a blueprint;
② Repair Tool on wreck circuit boards — approach a damaged circuit panel on a wreck, repair it, sometimes gives a new blueprint;
③ Read PDA data logs — some blueprints auto-unlock through story events.
Summary: find wrecks, scan everything, read every PDA entry — blueprints pile up naturally.

图纸解锁有三种方式：
① 扫描仪扫描残骸碎片——在水下找到飞船残骸的碎片，靠近后用扫描仪扫，扫满4片解锁一张图纸；
② 修复工具修复残骸电路板——靠近有损坏电路板的残骸，用修复工具修复，有时会给新图纸；
③ 读 PDA 里的数据记录——有些图纸通过故事事件自动解锁。
