Can I make Вода (Water) without killing Вода (Water) Slugs?

Да. Process Волокнистая масса (Fibrous Pulp) as the non-lethal alternative; players use farmed Губка-макарон (Macaron Sponge) as a renewable источник. The Вода (Water) record lists both recipe paths.

How can I cross the Collector canyon without losing my «Головастик» (Tadpole)?

Take the far south/right edge, hug the red Early Access boundary without crossing it, and keep the «Головастик» (Tadpole) moving at full speed. Players report two workable lines: skim the surface, or stay low under the outer crest and use terrain as cover. Do not stop in open water; if spotted, break line of sight behind the ridge before repairing. This reduces risk but does not guarantee a clean crossing after the more aggressive EA 1.1 Collector behavior.

What is the personal storage box actually useful for?

Fabricators do not pull materials from it. Players keep one seed copy of each farmable mineral there so crafting cannot consume the last piece; it also separates personal items in multiplayer.

How do I bioscan the Левиафан-коллекционер (Collector Leviathan) safely?

Equip the Camouflage passive biomod, approach in a «Головастик» (Tadpole), and exit as the Collector attacks the vehicle. Swim behind it, finish the scan, then stop moving so Camouflage can hide you until it loses interest. Distraction Flares are another repeatedly reported option; leave the «Головастик» (Tadpole) at the trench edge if you do not want to risk vehicle damage.

How do I permanently expand my inventory and hotbar?

Find the hacked, open biobeds inside colonist bunkers and interact with the green DNA terminal. These beds apply permanent inventory upgrades, and later upgrades also add hotbar slots; they do not change your respawn point. If you missed them, use the Anomaly Сканнер (Scanner)'s Biohacking Chamber target to locate the relevant bunkers.
