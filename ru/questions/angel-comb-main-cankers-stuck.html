Current answer

Install EA 1.1 Hotfix 4, restart the game client, and temporarily disable any mods before loading the save. The official patch specifically fixes Angel Combs not opening and blocking progression. If it remains stuck after confirming the hotfix is installed, report the save through in-game feedback or Неизвестно Worlds support.

Граница доказательств

Неизвестно Worlds Hotfix 4, published July 14, 2026, explicitly lists this progression blocker as fixed and advises disabling mods after updating. The original and follow-up Reddit reports confirm the pre-hotfix symptom, but they do not establish a separate manual recovery method.
