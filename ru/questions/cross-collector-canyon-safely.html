How can I cross the Collector canyon without losing my «Головастик» (Tadpole)?

↑

↳

Current answer

Take the far south/right edge, hug the red Early Access boundary without crossing it, and keep the «Головастик» (Tadpole) moving at full speed. Players report two workable lines: skim the surface, or stay low under the outer crest and use terrain as cover. Do not stop in open water; if spotted, break line of sight behind the ridge before repairing. This reduces risk but does not guarantee a clean crossing after the more aggressive EA 1.1 Collector behavior.

Граница доказательств

The original poster completed the crossing but was grabbed and arrived with 9% vehicle health. The boundary and terrain routes are repeated community advice; Collector AI is patch-sensitive, so this remains partial.
