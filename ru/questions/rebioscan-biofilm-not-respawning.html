Why isn't Biofilm respawning for a re-bioscan?

↑

Current answer

There is no reliable fix yet. Some saves respawn Моровой сок (Bloom Sap) but not Biofilm, while others differ. Check distant Cicada wrecks for missed scans and keep at least one Моровой сок (Bloom Sap), but treat the respawn behavior as unresolved.

Граница доказательств

Conflicting save-specific reports. This remains an open issue and is intentionally not featured as a solved answer.
