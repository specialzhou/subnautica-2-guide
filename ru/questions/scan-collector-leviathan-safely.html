How do I bioscan the Левиафан-коллекционер (Collector Leviathan) safely?

↑
205
↳
54

Current answer

Equip the Camouflage passive biomod, approach in a «Головастик» (Tadpole), and exit as the Collector attacks the vehicle. Swim behind it, finish the scan, then stop moving so Camouflage can hide you until it loses interest. Distraction Flares are another repeatedly reported option; leave the «Головастик» (Tadpole) at the trench edge if you do not want to risk vehicle damage.

Граница доказательств

The linked discussion contains repeated successful reports for Camouflage, «Головастик» (Tadpole) distraction and flares. Exact aggro range and timing were not measured and may change with AI updates.
