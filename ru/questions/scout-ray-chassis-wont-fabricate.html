Why won't the Шасси «Скат» fabricate?

↑
5
↳
7

Current answer

Make the Стыковочная шахта (Moonpool) much wider. After EA 1.1 the chassis can fabricate with its wings extended, so a pool that worked before may no longer provide enough clearance.

Граница доказательств

The original poster confirmed the larger Стыковочная шахта (Moonpool) solved the issue; no exact minimum dimensions are verified.
