Where can I find a Копейник (Sandspear) for the bioscan?

↑
3
↳
8

Current answer

Look for a tongue and spikes protruding from sandy cave floors. The original poster confirmed one below the Observatory; another player reported one in the cave beneath the coral dome by a wreck about 100 m northeast of the lifepod. The Запись Wiki also lists Заросшие руины (Overgrown Ruins), Плато (Plateaus), Мелководье (Shallows) and Холмы Некролеи (Necrolei Hills).

Граница доказательств

The biome range is Подтверждено Wiki. The route and visual cues are community-confirmed; this site still lacks an in-game screenshot and exact coordinates.
