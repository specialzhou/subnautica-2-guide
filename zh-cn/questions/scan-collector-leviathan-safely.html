How do I bioscan the 深渊巨触（Collector Leviathan） safely?

↑
205
↳
54

Current answer

Equip the Camouflage passive biomod, approach in a 蝌蚪号（Tadpole）, and exit as the Collector attacks the vehicle. Swim behind it, finish the scan, then stop moving so Camouflage can hide you until it loses interest. Distraction Flares are another repeatedly reported option; leave the 蝌蚪号（Tadpole） at the trench edge if you do not want to risk vehicle damage.

证据边界

The linked discussion contains repeated successful reports for Camouflage, 蝌蚪号（Tadpole） distraction and flares. Exact aggro range and timing were not measured and may change with AI updates.
